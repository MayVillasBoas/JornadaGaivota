## ENGLISH (about.astro)

About me

You can call me May. I work with technology, product, and people — and I think too much about how we should live our lives and make decisions.

I've always built things. An urban mobility startup in college, a jewelry ecommerce with my family, and even decision support systems using optimization algorithms for oil well drilling (yeah, quite the mix). I joined Stone when we had fewer than 50,000 customers — today that's less than what we onboard in a month. I left briefly to lead the financial services front at a software startup, and when I came back it was to help build the people team during a phase of insane growth. I watched the company go from 6,000 to 15,000 people, lose nearly 80% of its market cap, and rebuild itself. All from backstage.

I went back to my roots — product and artificial intelligence (except in 2014 we didn't call it that). Today I lead our commercial platform's AI-first transformation and build AI products for Brazilian small businesses.

The resume zigzags, just like life. In every challenge I've brought to the table what I do best — systems thinking that connects technology with real problems, and the drive to make things happen.
